Vikash

I'm a student who believes that the best life is one spent in curious pursuit — of ideas, of mastery, of meaning. I write to think clearly, and I build to understand deeply.

This site, Logicity, is my digital garden — a place where logic meets the city of ideas. Here I share what I'm learning in coding, what I'm thinking about philosophy, and which books are reshaping how I see the world.
